@def(preamble)
"\\documentclass["
"a5paper,ngerman"
"]{article}\n"
@end(preamble)
  • document is generated on a DIN A5 page format
  • with german spelling
  • and a small font

@add(preamble)
"\\fancyfoot[C]\x7b\x7b"
"\\color{deemph}\\small"
"$\\thepage$"
"\x7d\x7d\n"
@end(preamble)

  • \x7b and \x7d are curly braces
  • if they are inserted directly it breaks jekyll build
